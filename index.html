About

I run Rigo Consulting AB, a single-owner Swedish aktiebolag. I'm based in Sweden and have spent years working with software delivery, automation and infrastructure across a range of teams and organisations.

Rigo means 'blackbird' in Hungarian - a small, curious bird. That's roughly how I see myself: curious, observant and always noticing the small things.

Lately that's meant co-founding Tabete, running Rigo Consulting AB and working as a Continuous Delivery Enabler for embedded software at GN/Jabra.

I'm hands-on and like shipping real things. I prefer small experiments over big-bang changes and I care a lot about feedback loops, observability and making systems maintainable for the people who come after me.

I've often worked where software and hardware meet, so I'm used to environments that can't always use "normal" DevOps and continuous delivery practices.

Values

  • Simple over clever.
  • Automate what hurts.
  • Leave teams in a better place than I found them.
  • Care about both the flow of work and the people doing it.
